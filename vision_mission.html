Vision & Mission

"To create a community where students are not just learning to code, but coding to learn, innovate, and solve real-world problems. We envision G R I D as a launchpad for future tech leaders."

Our Guidelines

Inclusivity

Open to all branches and years. No prerequisite knowledge required, just curiosity.

Consistency

Regular meetups, contests, and coding streaks are encouraged.

Mentorship

Seniors guide juniors. Alumni guide seniors. A cycle of giving back.
